Riddle

Six minimalist SVG logo directions built from the handwritten R idea: quiet paper, dark ink, and a small hint that the page can absorb what you write.

Logo Generator Skill / SVG Set

01 Absorbed Tail

Organic / mnemonic

Classic handwritten R with three fading ink dots. This is the most direct bridge between “brand mark” and the page-drinks-ink mechanic.

02 Monoline Pencil

Clean / app-native

One continuous stroke, fewer accents, strongest small-size readability. It feels closest to Apple Pencil without becoming an illustration.

03 High Contrast

Premium / high contrast

A light R on a solid ink block. It is the most formal and highest contrast, useful if the app icon needs a stronger silhouette.

04 Open Page

Literal / diary

Adds the minimum book/page cue around the R. More explanatory, slightly less minimal, still clean enough for an iPad demo icon.

05 Ink Bloom

Atmospheric / soft

Uses a very quiet bloom behind the R, enough to suggest absorbed ink without turning into a stain or fantasy effect.

06 Stroke System

Constructed / technical

Breaks the R into visible stroke pieces, nodding to the app's handwriting synthesis pipeline while staying abstract.

Recommendation: use 02 as the base if you want the cleanest iOS icon, or 01 if you want the app's “paper absorbs ink” idea to be visible at first glance. 05 is the best hybrid candidate: simple R, slight ink atmosphere.
