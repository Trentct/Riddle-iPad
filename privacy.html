Privacy Policy

Last updated July 13, 2026

What Riddle sends

Only after explicit consent, the current handwritten page is sent as JPEG, or a keyboard note as text, to generate a reply. Up to three recent rounds of lightweight text context may accompany it. Older handwriting images are not sent again.

AI service and relay

Release requests pass through Riddle’s relay to Moonshot AI. The relay protects the upstream credential, applies quota and purchase verification, and does not intentionally persist page images or conversation content. Moonshot processes request data under its own policies.

On-device data

Reply context and up to 300 pages may remain in the local app container. Riddle does not sync this data to iCloud.

Identifiers and purchases

A random per-install UUID enforces the daily free allowance. Paid requests send an Apple-signed StoreKit transaction bound to that UUID. The relay verifies bundle, product, binding, expiration, and revocation without permanently caching the credential.

Your controls

You may withdraw consent, forget character memory, delete or export pages, or delete the app to remove its local container. Apple controls billing, refunds, and purchase history.

Tracking and children

Riddle has no advertising or analytics SDK and performs no cross-app tracking. It is not designed for children; AI output is not completely predictable.

Contact

Use Riddle Support without including journal content or sensitive credentials.

隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 7 月 13 日

发送的数据

仅在你明确同意后，当前手写页会以 JPEG 发送，键盘便笺则以文字发送；请求可能携带最近三轮轻量文字上下文，旧手写图片不会再次发送。

AI 与本地数据

正式版请求经 Riddle 中继交由 Moonshot AI 处理。中继不主动持久化纸页或对话。App 本地最多保存 300 张纸页，不同步到 iCloud。

标识符与购买

随机安装 UUID 用于免费额度。付费请求会发送与该 UUID 绑定的 Apple 签名交易并逐次验签，不永久缓存凭证。Riddle 不含广告或分析 SDK。

你的控制与联系

你可以撤回同意、遗忘角色、删除或导出纸页，也可删除 App 清除本地容器。请通过 Riddle Support 联系，且不要提交日记内容或敏感凭证。
