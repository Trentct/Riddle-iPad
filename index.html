Support

Help for the iPad journal that writes back.

Contact

Open a support request on GitHub Issues. Include device model, iPadOS, app version, and reproduction steps. Never paste private journal text, handwriting images, purchase credentials, or other sensitive information into a public issue.

Purchases

Use Settings → Restore Purchases on the Apple Account that made the purchase. Billing and refunds are handled by Apple.

Data controls

Settings lets you withdraw AI sending consent, forget character memory, delete or export pages, and enable the journal lock. Deleting the app removes its local container.
